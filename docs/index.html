A live market for one stock

“HELX” is a pretend company. Its price moves whenever someone buys or sells its shares — just like a real stock.

The order book is who's waiting

Buyers list the most they'll pay. Sellers list the least they'll accept. Two lines of people waiting for a deal.

iii

A trade happens when they meet

When a buyer's price reaches a seller's, shares change hands — that's a trade, and it sets the new price. Try it below.
